## Create your first view

<Steps>
1. Open the Trendz home page and click **Create View**.

2. Select **Line** as the chart type.

3. From the left panel, drag a **Date** field into the **X axis** slot.

4. Drag any telemetry field into the **Y axis** slot and click **Build Report**.

   At this point, the chart shows the average measurement across all entities in ThingsBoard.

5. Add **Entity Name** to the **Series** slot to split the chart into one line per entity.
</Steps>
