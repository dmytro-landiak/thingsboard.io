## v1.13.2 (Jun 27, 2025)

**Improvements:**

- Redesigned anomaly autodiscovery tasks.
- Added job for saving anomaly scores as telemetry in ThingsBoard.
- Added ability to create alerts based on discovered anomalies.
- Added new filter conditions — "not in" and "does not contain".

- Fixed fill gaps strategy during anomaly detection.
