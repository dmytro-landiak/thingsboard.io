This guide describes how to activate Trendz in your ThingsBoard Cloud environment via the Billing page.

<Aside type="note">
  Trendz Analytics is already enabled on the **free Cloud plan** — no activation steps are required.
  Follow this guide only if you are upgrading or changing your plan.
</Aside>

## Activation steps

### Step 1. Open the Billing page

<Steps>

1. Log in to your ThingsBoard Cloud environment.

2. In the left-side navigation menu, open the **Plan and Billing** page.

</Steps>

### Step 2. Add the Trendz add-on

**Option A: Add Trendz add-on only**

<Steps>

1. Click the **Add to plan +** button in the Trendz Analytics row.

2. Click the **Next: Summary** button.

</Steps>

]} />

**Option B: Change subscription plan and add Trendz add-on**

<Steps>

1. Click the **Update plan** button.

2. In the modal that appears, select the most suitable plan for your business goals and click the **Customize** or **Select** button.

3. In the Customize step, enable the **Trendz Analytics** add-on.

4. Click the **Next: Summary** button.

</Steps>

### Step 3. Activate plan

<Steps>

1. Review your plan configuration to ensure everything is correct.

2. If you have a coupon code, click **Apply coupon** and submit it.

3. Click the **Update plan** button to activate your plan.

</Steps>

Activation takes effect immediately.

### Step 4. Access Trendz interface

You can access the Trendz interface using the following links:

- [Trendz Cloud (US)](https://thingsboard.cloud/trendz/)
- [Trendz Cloud (EU)](https://eu.thingsboard.cloud/trendz/)

## Pricing

Up-to-date prices for the Trendz add-on are shown on the Plan and Billing page as described in [Step 3](#step-3-activate-plan) above.
