This guide describes how to activate Trendz for a ThingsBoard self-hosted environment via the License Portal.

## Activation steps

### Step 1. Log in to the License Portal

<Steps>

1. Open the [ThingsBoard License Portal](https://license.thingsboard.io/login).

2. Sign in with your ThingsBoard account as a sysadmin.

3. Go to the **Subscriptions** tab or **Perpetual licenses** tab, depending on your subscription type.

</Steps>

### Step 2. Select instance and enable Trendz add-on

<Steps>

1. In the list of licensed installations, select the ThingsBoard instance for which you want to enable Trendz.

2. Click on it to open the instance profile.

3. Click **Manage Add-ons**.

4. Scroll down and enable the **Trendz Analytics** checkbox.

5. Click **Next** in the lower right corner.

</Steps>

### Step 3. Review summary and complete payment

<Steps>

1. On the Summary screen, verify the selected plan and full price.

2. If everything is correct, click **Update** and complete the payment.

</Steps>

### Step 4. Restart ThingsBoard

Restart the ThingsBoard service to fetch the latest subscription information.
