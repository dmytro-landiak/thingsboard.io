Trendz operates as an add-on inside ThingsBoard. Activation is handled through the ThingsBoard billing or licensing interface — the exact flow depends on how your ThingsBoard environment is hosted.

Choose the guide that matches your setup:

- **<DocLink product={props.product} path="activation/public-cloud">ThingsBoard Cloud (US / EU)</DocLink>** — activate via the Plan and Billing page in your cloud tenant.
- **<DocLink product={props.product} path="activation/private-cloud">ThingsBoard Private Cloud</DocLink>** — a fully managed, isolated cluster; contact us to get started.
- **<DocLink product={props.product} path="activation/self-managed">ThingsBoard Self-Managed</DocLink>** — activate via the ThingsBoard License Portal.

<Aside type="note">
  Trendz Analytics is available for **ThingsBoard Cloud** (all plans, including the free tier), **ThingsBoard Professional Edition**, and **ThingsBoard Private Cloud**.
  It is not available for the Community Edition.
</Aside>
